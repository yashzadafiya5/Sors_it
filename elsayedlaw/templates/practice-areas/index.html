Practice Areas

Why we are different?

Sales

Selling a house can be a stressful time especially with the process of moving. Here at Elsayed Law we work at removing all the complications and taking care of the entire legal process from beginning to end. This includes adjusting property taxes, paying out mortgages, debts or liens, exchanging keys and signing closing documents with the client.

Purchases

Purchasing a home does not have to be overwhelming. If you are a first time homebuyer that meets requirements, we ensure that you receive the Land Transfer Tax rebate. At Elsayed Law, we take care of the legal requirements from beginning to end. This includes all the mortgage instructions, title searches, ensuring a clear title, down payment, registering the Title and mortgage and all legal documents for purchasing the property.

Refinancing

Whether you’re replacing an old mortgage with a new mortgage, placing a mortgage on a mortgage free home, renewing your mortgage, or securing a second mortgage, we are here to help you with the process.

Student visas/work permits

Apply to study in Canada as an international student, extend your study permit and find out about working while you study or after you graduate.

Permanent Residence

There are many different doors to permanent residency in Canada. Depending on your circumstances, you may be eligible through one of these doors. In order to best assess your chances, it is important to discuss and consult with a lawyer.

Notary

We will commission or notarize your documents and scan them to you for one less thing to worry about.
